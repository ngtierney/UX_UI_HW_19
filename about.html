Hi! I'm Nicole.

I am a UX/UI designer based in Jackson, Hole, Wyoming.

I was raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, but have spent many of my adult years in Minneapolis. I moved to Jackson Hole in May of 2019, pre-pandemic. It turned out to be a rather idyllic place to weather the global challenges, and before I knew it it felt like home.

Jill of all trades, master of some. I have tested my hand at apparel design, copywriting, photography, and mountaineering. On a weekend, you'll catch me in the mountains gathering inspiration from the natural world, and giving my calf muscles a run for their money alongside my partner and our pup, Orla.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Photography from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, and a post-graduate certificate in UX/UI Design via the University of Minnesota.
